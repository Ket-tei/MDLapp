##MDL Application

*Still in dev*

The project is to create an Mobile App to help the Student Organization of my former high school.

The main feature of the App is to help gather informations on the students of the organization (Name, First Name, Phone Number, Email...), modify them and delete them if the student leave the association. This information will be displayed on the screen.
The App will have to display the status of the association room (open or closed), to display important messages of the organisation.
Another feature is to choose at the start if your are a simple student of the association, a staff or an admin of the organisation. Depending of that you will have to enter a password and you will not have access to every features.

Because it's just a Student Association, the budget for those dev thing is inexsitant. So the App must use only free tools.

On this differents constraints I choose to build the app with React-Native and use Google Sheet Online for the Database. It's not the greatest Database and it's not meant for that, but it's free, secure, easy to use with the Google API.

For the Graphique Chartes I didn't have any constraints because the association have none.
